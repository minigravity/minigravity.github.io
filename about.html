What is this Project about?

Mini Gravity © is non-profit startup for providing a robust educational community in Kashmir. The community will be comprised of various college students, and other skilled people who are intersted in helping out others. The community aims at providing a friendly environment among various college stdents from across the valley to discuss common problems, work together and finding optimal solutions. The main motto is to bring like-minded people from different colleges together, work together on projects and help others to grow. If you want to become the part of this awesome community, then Join us as Member .

In addition to this, we're also working on developing the huge community, comprised of various College Clubs and Communities in Jammu & Kashmir, irrespective of their specialities. By doing so, will result in the sharing of resources and potential, which could prove beneficial for the society as well. The community will also help the school students to know about, about modern technologies which they could learn early on rather than lagging behind in these fields. Awareness campaigns about carreer options beyond JEE/NEET cult is the need of an hour, which will be covered as well.If you find it intersting,then Merge your club/community with us.
