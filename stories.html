The Story of Fast Beetle

Brief Interview
Entrepreneurship/Startups
5 Minutes read.

Read Story

What is Fast Beetle about and why was it created?

FastBeetle is a messenger and a logistic company primarily focusing on empowering local businesses. It was created to solve a logistics problems for other e-commerce startups.

Tell us a little bit about the developing team of Fast Beetle?

FastBeetle was founded by two friends. Abid Rashid and Sheikh Sami Ullah. Abid with a Tech background and Sami with operation exp started this journey first from a scratch and with the passage of time the team grew to 10 in house team members which includes delivery executives and in house support team.

What difficulties you faced in executing this idea?

Since our business is a cashflow business, We mostly faced a tough challange in managing the cash and the payments but after our tech stack was in place. All the issues were solved.

Did you recieve any kind of support from any platform, if so brief us about?

No, We have not received any support. Our startup was bootstrapped.

How did your company tackle the competition in the market?

With the right use of technology and solving the cash management tackled the competition in the market.

How many employees are currently working in this company?

10-20.

What is the average turnover of your company?

More than 1 crore per annum. (about more than $1,40,000 per annum)

How does your company cope up with the disruptive environment of the valley?

Its hard for a logistic company to cope up with such disruptive environment. For us its a hard task to restart again but with a passionate team in place. We try to get back and back again. :)

What is the message you want to give to the young aspiring entrepreneurs of Kashmir?

Do not reinvent the wheel. If you solve the problem. Your startup can sustain you and 100 more
